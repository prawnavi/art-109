Prepare your ingredients in a bowl: cinnamon, cardamom, sugar, vanilla bean/extract, and mix. Add the CACAO NIBS last, and mix.

Grind mixture into a paste with a MORTAR & PESTLE. Form paste into a cake-like shape and let it cool in the fridge overnight.

While the milk is heating in a pot, use a knife to chop the chocolate cake into small pieces.

Whisk the chocolate pieces into the milk.

Pour hot chocolate into your favorite mug and enjoy!

TIP: You can store the remaining chocolate paste in the fridge for future hot-chocolate making!
